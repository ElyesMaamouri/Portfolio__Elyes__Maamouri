We have to tell You something

We have observed how many manufacturers and suppliers cannot reach their full potential and their growth is blocked.

Having been in the pet industry for over 20 years, we see again and again how many manufacturing companies are run by really great people who work tirelessly to do their best and make great products with huge potential.

But... although they struggle every day, they are still not able to position their product in more markets. They are held back by the reality of the international market and their growth stagnates.

Growth brakes

You want and you need to grow

Staff shortage

It is extremely difficult to find qualified employees with the right know-how who want to and can work in international sales. Affording them is even more difficult. You need a passionate salesperson who will support you and help you advance. Not a burden on the payroll.

Lack of language skills

Even though English is the world language, the reality is that many distributors in Europe and other continents still do not speak English. This will quickly limit your potential sales

Cultural differences

Ethics and culture, business organization and negotiation management differ from country to country. If you do not know these, it is difficult to build long-term and trusting partnerships.

Lack of market knowledge

Without knowledge of the 4 P's, no manufacturer can be successful in the international market in the long term. Pricing Policy - Product Policy -Place - Promotion To gain this knowledge, it takes employee involvement and time... a lot of time, that often is lacking. The product is not suited to the needs of the market and the resources used - be it money, time or employee resources - slip away.

Missing contacts

It doesn't work without contacts -or it's just very, very difficult. Maybe you have a good employee who is motivated, wants to get involved in international sales and also speaks 1 or 2 foreign languages. But without an existing network of contacts, expanding international sales is extremely difficult.
